# Branding Ideas

## Mascots

1. **Dung beetle** rolling a polygon sphere — turns community complaints into polished tools
2. **Mantis shrimp** — perceives 16 color channels; sees what everyone else misses
3. **Tardigrade in a hard hat** — indestructible, microscopic, still showing up
4. **Hagfish** — does the unglamorous cleanup work no one else wants; surprisingly effective
5. **Naked mole rat** — pain-resistant, colony-minded, thrives underground
6. **Cleaner wrasse** — tiny fish that removes parasites from larger creatures; lives by eliminating irritants
7. **Bombardier beetle** — small, precise, produces an outsized reaction from almost nothing
8. **Tuatara** — 200-million-year-old reptile with a third eye; ancient sensor, still perfectly functional
9. **Rotifer** — microscopic filter-feeder shaped like a spinning wheel; processes enormous volumes, keeps only what matters
10. **Blind cave axolotl** — navigates entirely by sensing vibrations; finds problems without being able to see them
11. **Pilot fish** — swims alongside giants, removes what irritates them, asks nothing in return
12. **Mycelium network** — no face, no center, just invisible connective tissue that quietly makes the whole forest work

## Taglines

1. *The 47th reply in a 3-year-old thread.*
2. *Someone had to.*
3. *The itch you stopped filing tickets about.*
4. *Pain detected. Addon deployed.*
5. *Built from the workarounds you memorized.*
6. *The internet complained. We took notes.*
7. *Turns out it's fixable.*
8. *Art tools, minus the friction no one reported.*
9. *Found in the comment sections.*
10. *Still broken in 4.2? Not anymore.*
11. *We read the replies so you don't have to redo the steps.*
12. *Automated empathy for people who just want to make things.*

## Favorites

- **Mascot:** Rotifer — spinning wheel + filter metaphor, does its work invisibly
- **Tagline:** *The 47th reply in a 3-year-old thread.*
