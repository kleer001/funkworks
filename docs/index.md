## Addons

### [Selective Edge Split](selective_edge_split)

Split panel gap edges without touching your render sharps. Tag edges once with Ctrl+E, apply a scoped split when ready — no clearing and re-marking required.

**Blender 4.0+** &middot; Free

---

### [Fluid Domain Auto-Visibility](fluid-domain-visibility)

One-click visibility keyframing for fluid simulation domains. Automatically hides the domain box before your sim starts — no manual keyframing required.

**Blender 4.0+** &middot; Free

---

## Houdini

### [Scale COP](scale_cop)

Resize and reposition an image in Copernicus with independent fit mode, tiling, and resampling filter.

**Houdini 20+** &middot; Free
